How to Explore the Pages
Zoom — scroll the mouse wheel, pinch on touch, or use the + / − buttons in the toolbar. Press 0 to reset.
Pan — once zoomed in, click and drag (or swipe with one finger) to move around the page.
Zoom Lens — tap the magnifying glass button (far left of the toolbar) or press L, then hover the page for a magnified close-up.
